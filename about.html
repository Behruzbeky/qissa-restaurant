Our Story

QISSA means "tale" in the ancient languages of Central Asia. We built this restaurant on a simple belief — that food is the oldest form of storytelling.

Born in the Tandoor

Every dish we serve carries centuries of tradition — passed down through generations of Uzbek families who understood that a meal is never just a meal.

Our chefs work the clay tandoor the slow, patient way, locking in flavour that tells you something true about the land this food comes from.

"We don't just cook for our guests. We tell them where they come from, one plate at a time."

— THE QISSA KITCHEN
