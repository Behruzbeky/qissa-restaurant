ABOUT QISSA

A restaurant built on tradition

A Story Born in the Tandoor

QISSA — which means "tale" in the ancient languages of Central Asia — is a restaurant built on the belief that food is the oldest form of storytelling.

Located in the heart of Tashkent, every dish we serve carries centuries of culinary tradition — passed down through generations of Uzbek families who understood that a meal is never just a meal.

Our chefs specialize in tandoor cooking — the slow, patient method that locks in flavour and tells you something true about the land this food comes from.
